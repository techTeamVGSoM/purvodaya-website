About Purvodaya

Purvodaya, the annual B-school fest of Vinod Gupta School of Management, IIT Kharagpur is an amalgamation of events spread across all the domains of business and management. Spread across 3 days, it provides an ideal platform for the budding managers and innovators of today to hone their skills and test their mettle against their peers. The event hosts a complete business extravaganza.
We create an environment wherein students from all over India aspire to learn the business jargons and win the competitions. It aims to bring together management students from various B-schools of the country to a common platform where each participant presents his/her business acumen to be the best and win the competitions.
A coalescence of corporate tycoons, industry experts, technocrats and best business minds from all over India bring their vast knowledge share to the table The Speakers are creative market leaders, who share their insights at the leadership summit. Meeting the experts unlocks the secrets of commercial success. Together, they change how we think about business.
Purvodaya is the place to learn, share and develop new ways of thinking. It is a festival of ideas, innovation, challenge and creativity, a place where future businessmen and corporate leaders of different backgrounds come to grow.
Purvodaya concludes on an ecstatic note with a fun-filled cultural night thereby adding colors to the mundane MBA life.

Purvodaya 2022 – BREAKING THE PARADIGMS

“If you can’t fly then run, if you can’t run then walk, if you can’t walk then crawl, but whatever you do you have to keep moving forward.”

- Martin Luther King Jr

Dating back to Robert Bruce and spider's story to modern-day, Arunima Sinha climbing Mount Everest beating all odds, one thing that lies common in their success stories is that they all failed in their respective endeavours only to bounce back with even more zeal to win. Would we have ever gone to the moon if we gave up?
Purvodaya, the annual B-School fest of VGSoM, IIT Kharagpur, is an epic saga of perseverance where competitions will provide a platform to test out the budding manager's adaptability, flexibility, and robustness. The prodigious mix of events spanning across contemporary business problems from a diverse spectrum of industries challenges the finest B School students of our country to show the world that with resilience, we can beat the odds and rise above the ashes.
The essence of Purvodaya has always been about drawing lessons from defeat, rather than boasting about our victories. We strive to continue the magic and the ethos that has made past editions events to remember, by making sure that Purvodaya is where ideas get sparked, innovations thrive, and creativity blooms.

Srijan - Present your Brainchild

Entrepreneurship Cell

Promoting the spirit of Innovation and Entrepreneurship amongst the students of B-schools across the country is the objective of Srijan – Present your Brainchild. This will provide a unique opportunity for budding entrepreneurs to pitch their business ideas with venture capitalists and investors.
Srijan is a business plan competition that will enable you to take your ideas to the next level. The viability of your ideas shall be judged, mentors will guide you to improve upon your business plans and you will have a chance to showcase your ideas and B-plans in front of B-Plan experts.

Vichaar – Business Learning Redefined

Entrepreneurship Cell

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) of Vinod Gupta School of Management, IIT Kharagpur proudly presents the prestigious event of Purvodaya festival, Vichaar – Business Learning Redefined. Vichaar provides a unique opportunity for budding entrepreneurs to apply their strategic skills in solving real-time business problems faced by various Startups.

Modus Operandi - The Supply Chain and Operations Competition

ScOpe Club

Here is the chance to win exciting rewards by discovering your acumen on Supply chain and Operations, with challenging and invigorating rounds.

Synergy – A Competition for Stock Market Enthusiasts

Sumantran - Hunt for the star consultant

The Consulting Club

The Consulting Club of Vinod Gupta School of Management, IIT Kharagpur is excited to present its annual event of the Purvodaya festival, Sumantran - A hunt for the star consultant. Here’s your chance to compete with the best minds across the nation. Put on your consultant's hats and make your way through quizzes, cases, presentations, and challenging questions to emerge as the star consultant. Spread across three rounds, the contest will challenge you with a comprehensive case that will test your analytical skills and business acumen to dive deep into the critical business problem and solve it in a structured way to deliver effective and implementable solutions.

Guesstimates – What gets measured, gets managed

The Consulting Club

The Consulting Club (TCC) of Vinod Gupta School of Management, IIT Kharagpur proudly presents one of the most prestigious events of Purvodaya festival, Guesstimates – What gets measured, gets managed. Consultants all over the world are required to guesstimate on a daily basis to propose the best possible solution for the clients’ requirements. Here is a chance to win exciting prizes by discovering your acumen on Guesstimates through challenging and invigorating rounds.

Leader’s Challenge - Rise of the Marketer

MAD Club

LEADER's CHALLENGE is a national level competition for developing innovative product lines for a market leader in a yet unexplored market segment. The event will be organized by MAD, the Marketing and Advertising Club of VGSoM, IIT Kharagpur. The objective of the event is to generate ideas about a new product for a market leader building on their strengths and brand values, and then accordingly make plans for brand positioning, risk analysis, feasibility and SWOT analysis, financial analysis and overall business and marketing strategy for it. The idea is to sensitize students to real life business situations and to develop their analytical and presentation skills. Through this competition we aspire to reach out to a wide network of Indian academic institutes with the aim to foster and nurture the budding minds.

Think Vent

MAD Club

Think Vent is a fun and engaging event organised by the Marketing and Advertising Club of VGSoM, designed to test the innovation, creativity and brand awareness of the participants. The competition will offer a chance to explore the challenges faced by a brand manager or product marketer in the present era.

Parishram - Social Empowerment Case Study Challenge

Saamanjasya Club

Saamanjasya Club of Vinod Gupta School of Management, IIT Kharagpur proudly presents its prestigious event during Purvodaya, Parishram. A unique platform for students to showcase their analytical skills by solving a real-life CSR problem.
"Parishram" is a live Social Empowerment Case Study Challenge, being held as a part of Purvodaya 2021- our annual B-School fest - where we invite NGOs to participate by giving inputs as to the major issues the NGOs are facing in today's time. This will be further built into a Case Study, which will then be given to the participants from reputed B-Schools who will be working on solving the case and coming up with feasible solutions and future course of action for the same. In this way, we get to learn about the challenges that NGOs are facing today, and the NGOs would get some insights from the best minds of the country.

Aalekh - The Article Writing Competition

Saamanjasya Club

Saamanjasya – The CSR club of VGSoM, IIT Kharagpur presents an opportunity to do some mind scribbling and pen down your thoughts for the article writing competition.
A topic on a social issue will be provided to the registered candidates. The most practical, relevant, optimal, easy to implement and appealing solutions will be rewarded. Though English vocabulary alone is not the only criteria but the thought process and the structure of the article to be preferable maintained.
