About Purvodaya

Purvodaya, the annual B-school fest of Vinod Gupta School of Management, IIT Kharagpur is an amalgamation of events spread across all the domains of business and management. Spread across 3 days, it provides an ideal platform for the budding managers and innovators of today to hone their skills and test their mettle against their peers. The event hosts a complete business extravaganza.
We create an environment wherein students from all over India aspire to learn the business jargons and win the competitions. It aims to bring together management students from various B-schools of the country to a common platform where each participant presents his/her business acumen to be the best and win the competitions.
A coalescence of corporate tycoons, industry experts, technocrats and best business minds from all over India bring their vast knowledge share to the table The Speakers are creative market leaders, who share their insights at the leadership summit. Meeting the experts unlocks the secrets of commercial success. Together, they change how we think about business.
Purvodaya is the place to learn, share and develop new ways of thinking. It is a festival of ideas, innovation, challenge and creativity, a place where future businessmen and corporate leaders of different backgrounds come to grow.
Purvodaya concludes on an ecstatic note with a fun-filled cultural night thereby adding colors to the mundane MBA life.

“The past, like the future, is indefinite and exists only as a spectrum of possibilities.”

- Franklin D. Roosevelt

In today's dynamic and fast-paced world, the ability to navigate change and explore uncharted territories is paramount. In every industry, from finance to technology, from marketing to entrepreneurship, the horizon is continually shifting. What was once considered the cutting edge is now the norm, and what lies ahead remains a tantalizing mystery. To define a horizon is to limit, but to venture beyond the confines of fear, doubt, and uncertainty one must remember that the end of a horizon is the beginning of the other. The theme 'Navigating New Horizons' represents our collective commitment to growth, innovation, and the pursuit of excellence. It encourages us to break free from the confines of the familiar and embrace the challenges and opportunities that lie beyond. As we explore new horizons together, we'll discover that the end of one journey is only the beginning of another. Purvodaya'24 is your platform to set sail towards uncharted waters, to dream big, and to redefine what's possible. Join us in this exhilarating pursuit, and let's navigate new horizons, hand in hand, towards a brighter, more promising future.

Srijan - Innovation and Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship Cell

Promoting the spirit of Innovation and Entrepreneurship amongst the students of B-schools across the country is the objective of Srijan – the Annual Business Plan Contest. This will provide a unique opportunity for budding entrepreneurs to pitch their business ideas to venture capitalists and investors. Srijan is a business plan competition that will enable you to take your ideas to the next level. The viability of your ideas shall be judged, mentors will guide you to improve upon your business plans and you will have a chance to showcase your ideas and B-plans in front of angel investors and venture capitalists.

Sumantran - Hunt for the Star Consultant

This is your opportunity to compete with some of the brightest minds nationwide. Put on your consultant's hat and navigate through quizzes, case studies, presentations, and challenging inquiries to establish yourself as the star consultant. Guesstimates – The solution often turns out more beautiful than the puzzle

The Consulting Club

The Consulting Club (TCC) of Vinod Gupta School of Management, IIT Kharagpur proudly presents one of the most prestigious events of Purvodaya festival, Guesstimates – What gets measured, gets managed. Consultants thrive on the art of the informed guess. Every day, they navigate incomplete data and complex scenarios to deliver solutions that make a real impact. Guesstimates isn't just about numbers; it's about unveiling the hidden logic behind seemingly impossible questions. It's about thinking creatively within constraints and building compelling narratives with limited information. Here is a chance to win exciting prizes by discovering your acumen on Guesstimates through challenging and invigorating rounds.

Leader's Challenge

MAD Club

Leader’s Challenge is the Flagship event of the MAD, Marketing and Advertising Club of VGSoM, designed to test the budding leader within you. Leader's Challenge is a national level competition for developing innovative product lines for a market leader in a yet unexplored market segment. The idea is to sensitize students to real-life business situations and to develop their analytical and presentation skills. Through this competition, we aspire to reach out to a wide network of Indian academic institutes with the aim to foster and nurture budding minds.

Parishram - A Social Impact Case Competition

Saamanjasya Club

Drishyam - Ideas that inspire change

Saamanjasya Club

Purvodaya proudly launches the eagerly anticipated Saamanjasya: Drishyam, an impactful CSR challenge inviting participants to craft and share compelling short videos addressing pertinent CSR issues with creativity, authenticity, and reverence. Participants are encouraged to showcase their vision through concise videos, limited to 2-3 minutes. The initial stage involves the creation of these powerful visual narratives, followed by the subsequent submission of an associated PowerPoint presentation in later rounds. This dynamic competition promises a platform for innovation, sparking meaningful discussions and inspiring positive change. Unleash your creativity and contribute to the greater good through Saamanjasya: Drishyam.
